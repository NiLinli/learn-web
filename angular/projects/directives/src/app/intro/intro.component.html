指令Directives

  • Angular 的模板是动态的。当 Angular 渲染它们的时候，会根据指令给出的指示对 DOM 进行转换
  • Components come with functionality that makes it easy to add views, but directives can have views too.
  • But we can attach behaviors to an element without a template by using a directive.
  • Components always have a view. Directives may or may not have a view.
  • If you choose to render a view (a template) in your Directive, you can have more control over how that template is rendered.
